Aircraft Cariers

Let's start with the Aircraft Carriers. The first thing to know about this class is they requier a basic skill that is Target prioritization. The reason for this is because when you play Aircraft Cariers AKA CV's you are going to encounter a thing known as AA anti aricraft fire. Which will cause damage to your aircraft which are you main armament used in attacking surface combatants. The skill Target Prioritization comes in handy because when you look at the mini map wich will give you all the information required in choosing which ships to go after. Things to consider when choosing a target are how many ships are located near your intended target? you don't want to attack several ships becasue the AA will be much stronger and harder to get an attack through. So I recomend when the Game starts you launch your strik aircraft which will be the Rocket planes and fly around the map and spot both the left and right flanks. This will then give you the information neccesary to determine what flank is most viable to attack (whichever flank has the least amount of ships.) Another crusial skill when playing a cv is knowing where to position your vessel. There are a few factors wich determine a good position for a cv such as 1 getting close to the battlefeild without being detecting the best way to do this is to sail your ship upto an island while staing behind it to hide you ship from being detected.
